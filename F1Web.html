Welcome to Fast Lane

Fast Lane is there to keep you informed of old time Formula One Drivers and their famous moments. Stay Tuned for new information!

Fernando Alonso

Fernando Alonso is considered as one of the most celebrated drivers in Formula One history, considering that he has been racing for over 17 years.
One of his most famous moments include; winning two World Championships with Renault in 2005 and 2006.

Kimi Raikkonen

Kimi Raikkonen, is known to be calm and have outstanding skill by winning the 2007 Formula One World Championship with Ferrari.

Nico Rosberg

Nico Rosberg made history by gaining the 2016 Formula One World Championship by having rivalry with Lewis Hamilton.
